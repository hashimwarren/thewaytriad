üìñ The books of Luke & Acts

The issues in our society, in our churches, and in our own hearts are no different than what Jesus and the Apostles faced in their day. So instead of reinventing the wheel, we will read the message for us found in Luke and Acts.

As we read and meditate, please ask yourself three questions:

  1. What does this teach me about Jesus' purpose?
  2. What does this teach me about the church's purpose?
  3. What barriers would stop me from making this my purpose?
